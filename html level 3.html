<br> tag in html

<br> means break tag
it is used for break lines for text or other elements

Example 1:

<html>
<head>
<title> buttons </title>
</head>
<body>


<p> my name is sarath <br>
I am teaching HTML <br>
</p>

</body>
</html>

Output: (with break tag)

Without Break tag:

<html>
<head>
<title> buttons </title>
</head>
<body>


<p> my name is sarath
I am teaching HTML
</p>

</body>
</html>

Output: (Without break tag)
